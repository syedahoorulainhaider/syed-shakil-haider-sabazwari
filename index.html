Syed Shakil Haider Sabazwari

A Journey of Courage, Perseverance and Success

About Him

Syed Shakil Haider Sabazwari was born on 30 September 1972 in Rawat Radio Colony. He spent his childhood and youth in Rawat and lived there until the age of approximately 22–23 years. He completed both his early and higher education in Rawat and later earned a Master's Degree in Political Science. Today he is known as a respected businessman associated with professional work related to the Islamabad High Court.

His Early Life

Life was not easy for him. He lost his beloved mother when he was still a child. After her death, his father remarried. Growing up, he faced many hardships and challenges. His family consisted of three stepbrothers, two stepsisters, four biological sisters and a stepmother. Despite difficult circumstances and limited support, he continued to move forward with courage and determination. During those years, a few loyal friends became his greatest source of happiness and support.

The Struggle

Facing Hardship

He started life with very little support and had to rely on his own strength and determination.

Never Giving Up

He tried many professions and business ventures. Some succeeded, many failed, but he never stopped trying.

A Courageous Mind

Known for his intelligence and courage, he treated every failure as a lesson and continued moving forward.

Hard Work Pays Off

Years of hard work, patience and smart decisions eventually brought him respect, success and financial stability.

Marriage & Family

When he got married, he did not yet have a stable career. His wife stood beside him during difficult times and supported him when life was uncertain. Together they faced challenges with patience and faith. Eventually his dedication and hard work paid off, allowing him to build a successful life and provide comfort, security and opportunities for his family. Today he is the proud father of five children: • Three Sons • Two Daughters His greatest happiness has always been his family.

Personality

Syed Shakil Haider Sabazwari is known as: ✔ Intelligent and knowledgeable ✔ Courageous and determined ✔ Positive and optimistic ✔ Warm-hearted and caring ✔ Passionate about helping his family ✔ Strong-willed and resilient His journey inspires everyone around him to remain patient, hardworking and hopeful.

Hobbies & Interests

One of his greatest interests is cars. He enjoys learning about them and appreciates their design and performance. Above all, he loves spending his time, effort and energy doing whatever he can for the people he loves.
